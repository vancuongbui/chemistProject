The idea of this project is to keep track of the price of a wide range of 
products that attract customers from Vietnam who are shipping oversea a variety
of merchandise from Australia.
By doing so, it will help those buyers have great chance to buy products at cheap 
and at the right time since the project will inform them when a big offer is 
released.

Solution:
The Webpage will base on Django 2.0 and React Native
